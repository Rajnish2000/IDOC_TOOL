        Editable Math Formula:

        \[
        \exp\left[\int d^{4}x g\phi\bar{\psi}\psi\right]=\sum_{n=0}^{\infty}\frac{g^{n}}{n!}\left(\int d^{4}x\phi\bar{\psi}\psi\right)^{n}.
        \]
